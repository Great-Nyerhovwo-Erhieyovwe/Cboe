What is CBOE Global Markets (Cboe) and what does it offers?

Cboe Global Markets, Inc. (Cboe) is one of the world's largest exchange holding companies, providing cutting-edge trading and investment soluctions across a diverse range of asset classes, including equities, derivatives, FX, and digital assets.

Founded: 1973 (as the Chichago Board Options Exchange)

Headquarters: Chichago, Illinois, USA

What Cboe Does:

  • Options Trading
  • Equities Trading
  • Global Derivatives
  • Cboe Votality Index(VIX)
  • Foreign Exchange (FX)
  • Digital Assets
  • Market Data & Analytics
